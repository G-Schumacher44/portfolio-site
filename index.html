About Me

For 15 years, I managed operations and led teams, making critical decisions based on the data I was given. I learned firsthand that the most powerful insights aren't found in raw numbers, but in the stories they tell—stories that reveal deeper meaning, both in business and in life. This realization drove my pivot from a consumer of data to a creator of data-driven narratives.

My background gives me a unique perspective. I don't just see tables and columns; I see the business challenges and stakeholder questions behind them. My work is about bridging the gap between complex technical analysis and the clear, actionable insights that empower teams. Whether it's building a dashboard, an ETL pipeline, or a machine learning model, my goal is to translate data into a compelling story that resonates.

This philosophy is the common thread in my portfolio, from the narrative-driven modules in the SQL Stories Ecosystem to the practical guides in the Analyst Resource Hub. I build tools and tell stories that help others see their work in a new light.

Based in the Midwest • Open to remote/contract roles
