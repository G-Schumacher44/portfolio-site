About Me

Throughout my career, I’ve managed operations and led teams, making decisions from the data in front of me. I discovered that the real value of data isn’t in raw numbers—it’s in the story they tell and the understanding they reveal. That realization transformed me from a consumer of data into a creator of data-driven narratives.

My operations background means I don’t just see tables and columns—I see the business challenges and stakeholder questions behind them. I bridge the gap between technical analysis and clear, actionable insights that empower teams. My goal is to deliver clarity that informs decisions and ignites action.

This philosophy is the common thread in my portfolio, from the narrative-driven modules in the SQL Stories Ecosystem to the practical guides in the Analyst Resource Hub. I build tools and tell stories that help others see their work in a new light.

Based in the Midwest • Open to remote/contract roles
