Dirty Birds Data Generator

Synthetic penguin sight–resight datasets for EDA, QA, and modeling — with realistic messiness profiles.

System Summary

Inspired by Palmer Penguins, Dirty Birds generates configurable sight–resight datasets with randomness and controlled data quality challenges. It’s a practical sandbox for teaching, QA, and prototype modeling.

What Does This Help Solve?

Ecological datasets are often messy, limited, or unavailable — making it difficult for analysts and students to practice with realistic data.

The Dirty Birds Data Generator addresses this by:

  • Producing synthetic tagging datasets at scale
  • Modeling sight–resight behavior to mimic longitudinal field studies
  • Supporting teaching, QA, and prototyping across tools and workflows

History

The project began as a playful riff on Palmer Penguins. While learning SQL, I was repeatedly exposed to versions of that dataset and wanted something more scalable. What started as a simple experiment to generate larger data has evolved into a flexible simulator for practicing SQL, testing pipelines, and experimenting with data science tools — all while emphasizing simulation and reproducibility.

Future State

  • Additional species templates.
  • Richer messiness (sensor errors, missing bands).
  • Multi‑year study simulations.
