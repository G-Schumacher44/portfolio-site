Case Studies

Retail Returns Diagnostic for VP of Sales

Problem: A growing retailer saw refunds eroding 21% of net revenue with no visibility into which channels, regions, or products were driving the losses.

Delivered: End-to-end SQL analysis, a live Looker Studio dashboard, and an executive report identifying root causes across channels, shipping methods, and customer segments.

Impact: Pinpointed $10.6M in refund exposure, flagged the highest-risk channel (29% refund rate), and provided a prioritized action plan to reduce returns below 20%.

Inventory Efficiency Audit

Problem: An e-commerce retailer had $19.1M in capital locked up in under-utilized inventory with no systematic way to identify which SKUs to prioritize.

Delivered: A SQL-based audit framework with a weighted attention scoring system, interactive workbook with drill-down filters, and an executive summary with tiered recommendations.

Impact: Identified 562 under-utilized SKUs, quantified $3.8M in recoverable capital, and projected $0.8M-$1.1M in annual carrying cost savings.

SQL • Python • Data Storytelling

Customer Retention Strategy

Problem: A marketing team needed a retention snapshot but had no cohort-level visibility into churn timing, loyalty tier performance, or channel-specific lifetime value.

Delivered: Cohort retention heatmaps, first-to-second purchase conversion analysis, loyalty tier diagnostics, and channel CLV segmentation with an executive report.

Impact: Revealed an 11-point retention drop in the first 90 days, identified 0% repeat rate in the Bronze loyalty tier, and showed Phone channel drives 58% of high-CLV customers.
